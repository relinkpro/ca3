About ReLink

A fictional college project about reclaimed building materials in Ireland.

What is ReLink?

ReLink is a fictional website created as part of a college web design project. It is designed as an online marketplace where sellers can list reclaimed building materials, buyers can browse and purchase them, and drivers can arrange delivery. The idea is to connect all three users in one simple website.

Purpose of the Website

The purpose of ReLink is to show how a simple website could help reduce waste from construction and renovation projects. Instead of throwing away surplus materials, sellers can list them for others to buy. Buyers get cheaper materials and drivers get flexible delivery work. Everyone benefits from one transaction.

Reuse and Sustainability

A lot of building materials are thrown away even when they are still in good condition. ReLink is based around the idea of reusing these materials instead of sending them to landfill. This is better for the environment and can also save money for both sellers and buyers.

Benefits of ReLink

  • Sellers can make money from leftover materials instead of paying for disposal
  • Buyers can find good quality reclaimed materials at lower prices
  • Drivers can pick up flexible delivery jobs that suit their schedule
  • Less building waste ends up in landfill
  • Everything is handled through one simple website
  • Works for small businesses, tradespeople and individual buyers

Our Three Users

Sellers - Businesses and contractors with surplus materials.

Buyers - Renovators and tradespeople looking for reclaimed goods.

Drivers - Van owners and couriers looking for flexible work.

Where Materials Come From

Most of the materials listed on ReLink come from renovation sites, construction companies and warehouses that have leftover stock they no longer need.
