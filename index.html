Browse Materials How It Works

Who Uses ReLink?

Sellers

Businesses with leftover materials can list them instead of paying for disposal. A simple way to make money from surplus stock.

Buyers

Renovators and tradespeople can browse reclaimed materials at lower prices, with delivery options available.

Drivers

Van owners and couriers can pick up flexible delivery jobs through the site and choose when they want to work.
