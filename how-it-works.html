How It Works

A simple explanation of how ReLink would work for Sellers, Buyers and Drivers.

ReLink is a fictional website that connects three types of users. Sellers list materials they no longer need, Buyers browse the materials, and Drivers help with delivery. This page explains how each user would use the website.

Sellers

Sellers are businesses or contractors who have leftover materials they want to sell.

  1. Create a seller profile on the website
  2. Take photos and fill in details about the materials
  3. Set a price and post the listing on the website
  4. Arrange the sale and collection or delivery

Buyers

Buyers are renovators, tradespeople or anyone looking for reclaimed materials at a lower price.

  1. Browse the listings on the Browse Materials page
  2. Find a material that suits their needs and budget
  3. Contact the seller through the website
  4. Agree on collection or arrange delivery with a ReLink driver

Drivers

Drivers are van owners or couriers who want flexible delivery work.

  1. Sign up as a driver on the ReLink website
  2. Browse available delivery jobs in their area
  3. Accept a job that suits their schedule and location
  4. Complete the delivery job

Why ReLink is Useful

  • Sellers can list surplus materials instead of paying to dispose of them
  • Buyers can find good quality reclaimed materials at lower prices than buying new
  • Drivers can pick up flexible delivery work that fits around their schedule
  • Less building waste ends up being thrown away unnecessarily
  • All three users are connected through one simple website
